LIP CARE

The skin on your lips are thinner than any other body part making it verysensensitive and vulnarable if not taken proper care.

So heres a few tips to add to your lip care routine and achieve healthy and beautiful lips always.

MASTER THE ‘LIP GAME’

The Colorsoul range of Lipsticks are 100% vegan,contain natural ingredients, are enriched with the goodness of Jojoba Oil, Vitamin E, Almond Oil and SPF 15 to give your lips the best care and protection while making it look amazing.

The Prep

Before applying any lipstick, you need to make sure you have a clean and smoothbase which can be achieved by exfoliating the lips. Start off by scrubing your lips to removeall dead skin. Wipe your lips to ensure your lips are clean and ready for the final look.

The Lipstick

Start by applying the lipstick of your choice on the center of your upper lip. Follow your lipline for best accuracy and fill in your lips from the centre to both the corners. Repeat the same process for your lower lip. Repeat if necessary, but avoid over applying as this could result in product build up and make your lipstick look patchy.

MASTER THE ‘LIP CARE’

Lip care is essential for everyone you need a proper routineto achieve soft, supple lips. Here are a few tips on lip care for healthy and beautiful lips.

Exfoliate

Dry, chappy lips can indicate that your lips need more love and care. Exfoliating is essentialto achieve amazing-looking lips naturally. It eliminates the dead skin on your lips andstimulates circulation and blood flow making your lips plumpy and soft. Since over-exfoliatingcan cause swelling, sensitivity, and bleeding, you should exfoliate only once a week.

Treat

Treating your lips with the help of serums if there are signs of dullness or fine lineswill help achieve faster results. Lip Serum effortlessly penetrates the skin and can address mostlip concerns you might have.

Hydrate

Our lips are delicate and need constant hydration for the best lip health. Hydrationis the most important part of lip care. Moisturizing your lips consistently with natural products likeShea butter and drinking a lot of water help achieve beautiful and healthy lips.

Mask

To get the best of hydration, you can choose to apply a lip mask over your lip balm tolock in the moisture. Lip masks are usually thicker, it's usually best to use them at nightfor intense hydration.

protect

Your lips need constant protection from harsh sun rays. Always apply lip productswith SPF to protect your lips during the daytime. The Colorsoul range of lipsticksenriched with SPF 15 helps with the ultimate protection against the sun.
